# Project Review

## Project

**Name:** Carriage Run

**One Sentence Pitch:** Get your cargo safely to the next destination


# 1. First Impression
Spend 10-20 minutes using the project without making changes.
- the second map diffculty ramps up to fast, with a couple of upgrades it should have been more doable
- overall the gameplay felt pretty good
- current map movement is 'interesting' it feels less like the ground is moving then the caravan
- maybe a free roam option for the guards might be good, and direct when needed
- no clear sign what speed the player is going at
- to much information on the initial screen, I think other maps should be hidden until we are close to unlocking them
